Welcome to Learn Japanese Names

Japanese names can be difficult to get your head around.

The kanji are not always very intuitive, and it can take some time to put them into the context of an actual name.

The best way to learn is by doing, which is why we've created this site: to give you an easy and engaging way of memorising kanji used in names.
