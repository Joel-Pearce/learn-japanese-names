名の漢字を覚えよ!

Japanese names can be difficult to get your head around.

The kanji are not always very intuitive, and it can take some time to put them into the context of an actual name.

The best way to learn is by doing, which is why we've created this site: to give you an easy and engaging way of memorising kanji used in names.

This site is still in alpha, and as such only consists of the 100 most common Japanese names.

Our intention is to make this a free, open source site for learning and memorising the readings of Japanese names with the help of comprehensible input.

Cards are first available to be learnt, and then can be reviewed.
